BRIO

CONSOLE

NEW COLLECTION

L. 140 x H. 72 x D. 40 cm
(55.1"l x 28.3"h x 15.7"d)

Design: Sacha Lakic

The Brio collection features a contemporary and elegant aesthetic. The shapes created by the solid walnut pieces bring an aerodynamic look.

This console features a top either in glass (smoked or acid finish) or in a ceramic that resembles Noir Desir marble.

PRODUCT DETAIL
Console, structure in solid walnut, top in 8mm-thick smoked bronze or acid glass.

Manufactured in Europe.
