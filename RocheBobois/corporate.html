TRADE & CONTRACT DEPARTMENT

Roche Bobois Trade & Contract has the mission to offer premium furnishings - a result of more than 50 years of iconic design, unparalleled creativity and cutting-edge innovation, curated to the residential, commercial and hospitality sectors.

Roche Bobois Trade & Contract's furniture is designed to meet US codes and requirements particular to the hospitality and commercial environments, ensuring durability and longevity. We have COM and COL capabilities, and can also customize products to meet aesthetic requirements, commercial standards or value-engineered alternatives. A quick-ship program is also available for select pieces in our collection.

A new dedicated Roche Bobois Trade & Contract team ensures that design professionals can now recommend, order and include Roche Bobois furniture with design projects of every scale. We also offer furnishing packaging and innovative project solutions, home staging, hotel design, offices and lobby/foyer design, among other services.

BECOMING A ROCHE BOBOIS FRANCHEE

Becoming a Roche Bobois franchisee is becoming a partner of the world leader of high-end furniture design and retail. This means being part of exclusive collections, a successful business model and having the permanent support of an internationally renowned group. It means displaying collections in a showroom entirely dedicated to Roche Bobois, an essential condition for the qualitative and efficient presentation of our models.

YOUR CAREER AT ROCHE BOBOIS

With over 255 showroom locations worldwide in more than 55 countries, you will find international career prospects, a myriad of cultures and the location that best suits your needs within our global network.

At Roche Bobois, we seek to attract and recruit the most talented individuals, regardless of their level of experience. We recognize that different life experiences and viewpoints bring value to our business, and seek out people from diverse backgrounds who can be themselves and make a difference.

Roche Bobois offers flexible, exciting and rewarding career opportunities in many different areas: from Sales, Interior design, Draughtperson to corporate positions such as Accounting, Administration, Logistics or Marketing. Interested in joining our team? Apply now!



Roche Bobois is a world leader in furniture design and distribution. Working closely with renowned designers such as Marcel Wanders, Kenzo Takada, Ora Ito, Cédric Ragot, Sacha Lakic, Christophe Delcourt and Stephen Burks and with Haute Couture fashion houses such as Jean Paul Gaultier, Missoni Home and Christian Lacroix Maison, Roche Bobois offers a broad range of exclusive made-to-order designs, manufactured with a high level of customisation in small European workshops.
